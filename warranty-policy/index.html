Warranty Policy

Warranty Coverage

IT World The Computer ITSolution provides warranty on selected repair services and replacement parts depending on the type of repair performed. Warranty duration may vary based on components and repair category.

Warranty Period

Most repair services include a limited warranty of up to 6 months. The exact warranty period will be informed at the time of service depending on the repair type and components replaced.

What Is Covered

  • Issues related to the same repair performed
  • Replacement parts installed by our technicians
  • Manufacturing defects in replaced components

What Is Not Covered

  • Physical damage after repair
  • Liquid damage or accidental damage
  • Software issues unrelated to the original repair
  • Damage caused by third-party repair attempts

Warranty Claim Process

If you experience the same issue covered under warranty, you can contact IT World The Computer ITSolution and schedule a warranty inspection. Our technician will diagnose the issue and provide repair under warranty if applicable.
